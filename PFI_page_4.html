The DreamSMP

The Dream SMP is a survival multiplayer Minecraft server that is exclusively open to invited guests. It was created by YouTubers Dream and GeorgeNotFound, and stars other Minecraft content creators, who roleplay as fictionalised versions of themselves in a loose scenario.

Minecraft Championship

Minecraft Championship or more known as MCC is a Minecraft event organized by Noxcrew that invites many Minecraft content creators. They compete in teams of four against ten teams in various challenges that will test their abilities.

Link to the MCC website

Events

Twitch Rivals

Twitch Rivals is an esports event tournament hosted by Twitch. It features some Twitch streamers and former pro players, each teams must compete against each other in different competitive games.

Link to the Twitch Rivals website

Updates

Current Minecraft version

The current update is 1.18. This update radically redesign the Overworld generation with larger caves, bigger mountains, new cave biomes and flooded caves.

Next Minecraft Version

The Mangrove Forest will be introduced in Minecraft 1.19, as well as major changes to the Swamp biomes. The upcoming Deep Dark biomes will allow players to broaden their exploration into caves and the aforementioned underground cities. Sculk, a new block in Deep Dark, will be available.

What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a sandbox game where you can use your imagination to build whatever comes to mind.


Survival

In survival mode, the player must gather resources and construct structures while surviving in a randomly created world. You can engage with the locals villagers to trade and defend yourself against mobs that attack on sight at night.

Hardcore

In hardcore mode, it is just as survival mode but the player after one death cannot respawn. The difficulty of the game is also set on hard.

Creative

Creative mode removes the survival aspects of Minecraft. It gives players the access to every block in the game and also flying.
